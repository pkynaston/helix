## What you need to know

Digital Sovereignty for AEM Managed Services is a new offering that helps governments and regulated organizations meet strict jurisdictional requirements — data residency, cleared personnel, air-gapped environments, and compliance with frameworks like FedRAMP+, Protected C, GDPR, and IRAP.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Rapid Deployment** | Deploy into a new jurisdiction in as little as one week using automated tooling — versus months for on-premises or DIY cloud. |
| **Two Tiers** | **Prime** covers most sovereign use cases. **Ultimate** adds TS-SCI clearances, high-side deployment, and compliance customization. |
| **Pricing** | Prime is a 50% uplift on all AMS base SKUs. Ultimate is a 75% uplift. The uplift cannot be discounted. |
| **CSE Role** | Setup and configuration is managed in-region by an assigned Customer Success Engineer. You own the relationship. |

---

## Who to target

The best-fit customers are in public sector and highly regulated industries:

- Government agencies requiring data residency within national borders
- Healthcare, financial services, and defense organizations
- Critical infrastructure operators
- Multinationals operating across multiple jurisdictions with varying compliance requirements

If a customer mentions keeping data in-country, localized personnel, strict audit obligations, or high-side / air-gapped environments — Digital Sovereignty is the right conversation. If they only need stronger encryption or WAF/DDoS, Enhanced Security may be sufficient.
